SHAPOORJI PALLONJI, KANDIVALI

Prominent localities in Mumbai, such as Kandivali, have been attracting residents from all over the city. The strategic location of this area ensures a seamless connectivity to the workplaces and places of interest around the city. Check out these aristocratic homes at Sarova, a new estate developed in Kandivali. Here, you have elegant homes in 1.5, 2, and 3 BHK configurations. This is an ongoing project where investors are already booking their apartments. The Sarova price currently starts from INR 1.54 crore, and ranges up to INR 2.79 crore, depending on the configuration you choose. As the property value is rapidly escalating, this will be the right opportunity for you to buy these homes. The extensive plethora of lifestyle and entertainment amenities that residents can enjoy in this project makes it suitable for millennials and corporate professionals. You have a large clubhouse in this project, where lots of activities are available for the residents to engage in. It comes with a clubhouse, meditation centre, indoor games, landscaped gardens, and power backup. All the residential units are spacious, and you will appreciate the intelligent floor planning by the developers that optimize the available space. The common areas remain under CCTV cameras all the time to ensure the protection of the residents. You can park your car safely in the dedicated space in the complex. Other amenities for the residents include a lift and jogging track.
